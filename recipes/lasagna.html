Lasagna

Description

A yummy lasagna that anyone can make but I won't

Ingredients

  • italian sausage
  • ground beef
  • onion, garlic, tomatos
  • water
  • i mean there are a lot of ingredients listed

Steps

  1. Cook everything in oven until browned. Stir in crushed tomatos, water, and all kinds of stuff.
  2. Boil a large pot, put noodles in for 10 minutes. Drain noodles, rinse with cold water.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  4. Spread meat sauce on a dish, and some cheese. Make layers.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes. Cool it. Serve.
